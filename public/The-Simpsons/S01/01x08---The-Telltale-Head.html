FADE IN: 淡入：

BARNEY: Hey, that’s Homer Simpson.
巴尼：嘿，那是霍默·辛普森。

PRINCIPAL SKINNER: And his kid, Bart.
斯金纳校长：还有他的孩子，巴特。

BURNS: Let’s k*ll them! Bring them out!
伯恩斯：把他们干掉！把他们带出来！

OTTO: Off the kid, man! Off him!
奥托：放开孩子，伙计！放开他！

BARNEY: Let’s k*ll this guy!
